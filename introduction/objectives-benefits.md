# Objectives/Benefits



<details>

<summary>Establishing a platform for the collective benefit of all</summary>

DBrains aims to be a common good, developed and sustained by the community for the community. Empowering communities to self-organize, it aligns with the core principles of blockchain technology.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Federating and amplifying collective intelligence </summary>

DBrains fosters a collaborative environment where individual and collective interests align. By leveraging adapted tools and tokenomics, we empower teams to work efficiently and effectively. Our inclusive approach encourages diverse contributions from people with a wide range of backgrounds and skills. This diversity can lead to innovative solutions and unexpected breakthroughs

</details>

<details>

<summary>Creating a platform that promotes fair collaboration</summary>

Fairness is a cornerstone of DBrains. The tokenomics ensures equitable compensation for all contributors. Both investors and developers will receive a share of the projects profits proportional to their token holdings. ( See section [A simple yet poweful tokenomics](/broken/pages/sLo3N5VFxWnk54wJAY4R) ).

To enable decentralized work, fair evaluation of contributions is crucial. The most effective and meaningful approach is to delegate this responsibility to the project's DAO, Breaking down work into atomic units and relying on community consensus for validation ensures fair compensation and minimizes conflicts of interest. Blockchain technology provides an immutable record of these contributions, guaranteeing transparency and enabling automated reward distribution



</details>

<details>

<summary>Addressing Labor Market Inefficiencies</summary>



DBrains streamlines the hiring process by:

* **Direct Project-Talent Matching**: Project launchers post their needs on the Projects Board, and talented individuals from the DBrains community can directly apply.
* **Fostering Inclusivity and Collective Intelligence**:: By giving more people a chance, including less experienced individuals, projects can benefit from diverse skills, unexpected talents, and the opportunity for less experienced members to learn from more experienced ones.
* **Minimizing Onboarding Risk:** The DAO's token-based reward system ensures that only valuable contributors are retained. This reduces the risk of onboarding individuals who do not actively contribute to the project's success.



Projects launcher describe their project and the skills they look for in the Projects Board. Talents can be directly onboarded from the DBrains community and start to contribute. Inclusiveness is one to pillar of DBrains and collective intelligence:  by giving a chance to more people, projects may benefit from their unexpected skills and competences. If the DAO considers that the member 's contribution are not valuable, he will simply not earned token and eventually being dismissed if not contributing.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Mitigating conflicts of interest present in traditional companies</summary>

Unlike traditional startups with predetermined profit sharing, DBrains rewards contributors based on their actual contributions. By breaking down work into atomic units and relying on community consensus for validation, we ensure fair compensation and minimize conflicts of interest

</details>

<details>

<summary>A Collaborative Ecosystem for Shared Solutions and Skills</summary>

DBrains fosters a collaborative environment where members can share skills and solutions, maximizing efficiency and innovation.

* Skill Sharing and Multiplier Effect:
  * **Diverse Contributions:** Members can contribute to multiple projects, leveraging their skills to earn income from various sources.
  * **Knowledge Exchange:** By working on diverse projects, members gain exposure to different approaches and best practices, expanding their skillset.
* Solution Sharing and Innovation:
  * **Collaborative Problem-Solving**: Projects can collaborate on shared challenges, pooling resources and expertise to develop innovative solutions.
  * **Leveraging Existing Solutions:** Projects can adopt and adapt solutions developed by other teams, accelerating development and reducing redundancy.

By facilitating the sharing of skills and solutions, DBrains creates a vibrant ecosystem where innovation thrives and resources are optimized.



</details>
